# Multi-Domain Intelligence Suite: HR, E-Commerce, & Education

A comprehensive data science ecosystem demonstrating predictive modeling and analytics across three major corporate sectors: Human Resources, Online Retail, and Global Education.

##  Key Modules

### 1. Educational Analytics & Upskilling (`6` Series)
Analyzes educational trends to identify high-demand skills and top-rated learning paths.
* **Objective:** Understand enrollment drivers and difficulty-to-rating correlations.
* **Files:** `6.ipynb`, `6.csv`, `6.png`

### 2. E-Commerce Customer Intent (`5` Series)
Predicts shopper conversion and revenue probability using web session behavior.
* **Files:** `5.ipynb`, `5.csv`, `5.png`

### 3. Recruitment & Hiring Auditor (`4` Series)
Automates candidate screening using interview and skill scoring.
* **Files:** `4 (1).ipynb`, `4 (1).csv`, `4 (1).png`, `4 (2).png`

### 4. Employee Attrition & Retention (`2` Series)
Predicts turnover risk with high precision (93% ROC-AUC) and integrated DEI bias audits.
* **Files:** `2.ipynb`, `2.csv`, `2.png` through `2 (5).png`

### 5. Compensation Benchmarking (`3` Series)
Regression modeling for equitable salary structuring based on tenure and age.
* **Files:** `3.ipynb`, `3.csv`, `3.png`

##  Technical Implementation
- **Data Engineering:** Handling imbalanced classes (SMOTE), string-to-numeric conversion, and scaling.
- **Modeling:** Random Forest (Classification), Linear Regression (Forecasting), and Probability Simulators.
- **Ethics:** Mandatory Bias Audits and Feature Importance transparency.

##  Business Impact
This suite provides a 360-degree analytical framework for organizations to hire, pay, retain, upskill, and convert—bridging the gap between HR and Revenue operations.
